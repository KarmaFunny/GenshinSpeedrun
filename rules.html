Rules Page

How to Submit/Contact

Contact @bingbongkarma on Discord.

Any and all suggestions or ideas are welcome!

Include the following details in your submission:

  • Display Name
  • Type and Category
  • Time
  • Proof (Link to video)

Categories

  • Domains
    Start - Pressing start on the key
    Finish - Challenge completed (white star/flash appears)
    Consumables - You may not use any
  • World Bosses
    Start - Boss HP bar shows
    Finish - Boss HP bar disappears
    Consumables - You may not use any
    You may not use any skills or bursts before entering combat
    You may engage combat with a skill or burst
  • Spiral Abyss
    Time - Final displayed time taken away from 10mins
    Hall of Fame will be added for each patch's top run
  • Commissions
    Start - When you start the first commission
    Finish - When you receive rewards from Kathryn
    Consumables - You may not use any
    Overall - Time does not include loading time during teleportation
    Region - Can be any
    Portable Waypoints - Are allowed

Types

  • Info
    Each category will have all types available except commissions
    More types / challenges will be added in future
  • 4* Only
    You may only use 4* characters
    You may use 5* weapons
  • Unrestricted
    You may use any characters
    You may use any weapons
